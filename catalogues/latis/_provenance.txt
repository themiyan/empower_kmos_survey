LATIS — Lyman-alpha Tomography IMACS Survey (Newman et al. 2025, ApJS 281, 32)
  Zenodo record   : https://zenodo.org/records/15557327  (latis_v1.0.tar.gz, 3.6 GB)
  DOI             : 10.3847/1538-4365/ae0cc5
  Downloaded      : 2026-05-20 (full tarball fetched manually, catalogue extracted)
  Instrument      : Magellan/IMACS multi-slit, R~1000, ground-based.
  Fields          : CFHTLS D1, D2 (= COSMOS), D4 (1.65 deg^2 total).

Files kept here:
  latis_zspec.fits   7,408 rows — the spectroscopic redshift catalogue (used here).
  latis_spectra.fits.zip          — 1D spectra (1,222-pix wave/flux/error/model arrays).
  latis_ssr.fits / latis_tsr.fits — spectroscopic / targeting success-rate maps.
  latis_v1.0/                     — extracted tarball tree (mocks, IGM maps, etc.).
  LATIS_DR1_Demo_v1.0.ipynb       — official demo notebook.

latis_zspec.fits columns:
  name      target id, "DX-N" (field encoded in first 2 chars)
  field     'D1' | 'D2' (COSMOS) | 'D4'   (bytes, |S2)
  RA, Dec   degrees
  z, z2     primary / secondary redshift
  zqual     quality 0-4 (4 = very high confidence; secure = zqual>=3)
  isqso, isstar, badredux, manualz, tomo, brionly  boolean flags
  rmag, u-g, g-r   photometry; comments  spectral notes

Counts (this catalogue):
  zqual>=3 (all fields)      : 5,575
  D2 / COSMOS rows           : 3,519
  D2 secure (zqual>=3)       : 2,637  (z=2-3: 1,919 -> matches paper headline)
  D2 secure galaxies         : 2,460  (zqual>=3 & !isstar & !badredux)
