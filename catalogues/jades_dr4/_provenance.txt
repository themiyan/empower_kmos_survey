Key spec-z columns in HDU 1 ("Obs_info"):
  Unique_ID, NIRSpec_ID, NIRCam_DR3_ID, NIRCam_DR5_ID,
  PID, TIER, Tier_old, Field ('GS' or 'GN'),
  RA_TARG, Dec_TARG (target coords, deg),
  z_Spec     : best NIRSpec redshift
  z_Spec_flag: byte 'A'..'E' (A=highly robust S/N>5 emission lines in R~1000;
                              B=secure, C=probable, D=low-confidence, E=no good z).
  z_R1000    : gratings-only redshift (use when prism low-quality)
  z_PRISM    : low-res prism-only redshift
  MUV / MUV_u / MUV_l (absolute UV magnitude, optional)
