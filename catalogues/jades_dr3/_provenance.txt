JADES DR3 NIRSpec spectroscopic catalogues (Rieke+ 2023, D'Eugenio+25)
  VizieR catalogue : V/159  (JADES NIRCam & NIRSpec phot. & line fluxes DR1-DR3)
  Reference paper  : D'Eugenio et al. 2025, ApJS (DR3)
  Pulled on        : 2026-05-18 via astroquery.

Tables stashed here:
  jades_dr3_gsgrat.fits           (V/159/gsgrat, 2,525 rows, 81 cols)
  jades_dr3_gsprism.fits          (V/159/gsprism, 2,525 rows, 94 cols)
  jades_dr3_gngrat.fits           (V/159/gngrat, 1,561 rows, 83 cols)
  jades_dr3_gnprism.fits          (V/159/gnprism, 1,561 rows, 92 cols)

Key spec-z columns:
  NIRSpID, NIRCamID, TIER, PID, Field, RAJ2000, DEJ2000,
  zspec, f_zspec  (NIRSpec redshift and quality flag).

`gsgrat` / `gngrat` are the gratings (G140M/G235M/G395M/G395H) tables;
`gsprism` / `gnprism` are the CLEAR/PRISM low-resolution tables.
Each pointing usually has both a prism and a grating spectrum, so the same
source can appear once per table.
